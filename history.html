The First Team

The first Babson Softball team was in 1987. Shay Blanchette was one of the atheletes on the roster, and she now a professor at Babson College. She is also the faculty advisor for the current team. The original roster had fourteen players. They had a 9 game season with a record of 5-4.

Records

The furthest the Babson Softball team has gone is the Super Regional Tournament in 2017. They have had 6 total appearances in the NCAA Tournament.

Team 36

Today's team has a roster of sixteen atheletes. The team competes in a 40 game season. The team is led by the head coach Dave Canan, assisted by Jenna Putala and Amber Hazard. The team also has a strength and conditioning coach Erin Smith, and an athletic trainer Amari Bradshaw. The softball team is set up for success this season with all their amazing staff!

TRAITS

Babson Softball has a team mantra called "TRAITS". It stands for Trust, Respect, Accountability, Integrity, Toughness, and Selflessness. The team regularly meets to discuss how to be agood teammate. This has evolved into a culture of women making eachother better.
